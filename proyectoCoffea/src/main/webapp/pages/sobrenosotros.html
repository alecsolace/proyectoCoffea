ABOUT US
Our story begins in May 2022. It was here that Coffea opened its first store in Zaragoza, offering freshly roasted coffee beans, teas and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. We decided to create this company because we love coffee.
Our goal is to take our company far so that everyone can try our coffee. We believe that we can achieve it with a lot of effort and sacrifice, and, most importantly, teamwork. We are a family.
